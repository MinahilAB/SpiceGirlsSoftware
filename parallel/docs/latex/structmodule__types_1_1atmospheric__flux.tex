\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Derived type for storing calculated fluxes. 

Fluxes are stored on cell faces/interfaces, hence their dimensioning ({\ttfamily nx\+\_\+loc+1}, {\ttfamily nz+1}) often differs slightly from the state.
